* dracula theme

    Use t in (load-theme 'dracula t) to make sure that it wouldn't ask for confirmation at start-up.
    
    #+begin_src emacs-lisp
    (use-package dracula-theme
       :config
       (load-theme 'dracula t)
       :ensure t)
    #+end_src
